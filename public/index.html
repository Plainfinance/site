Plain Finance

Plain Finance is a cross-chain decentralized aggregate transaction network based on Polkadot, with high TPS and low fees. Users can provide strategies or follow-up strategies in PAN Market to conduct liquid market transactions of Defi projects, which can be visually summarized as the following three characteristics:

1. Spot and derivative product transactions with the best price and lowest slippage possible in the market

2. Defi investment arbitrage between DEX ecosystems can be realized

3. Users can run their own investment strategies through the API link Plain Market link
